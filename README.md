2. What We Collect and Why
2.1 Account Information
Email address and display name — required to create and identify your account.
2.2 Task and Productivity Data
Tasks — titles, notes, scheduled dates and times, complexity, categories, estimated duration, actual completion time, distraction counts, and completion status.
Recurring task definitions — schedules you configure for repeating tasks.
Mood entries — daily mood selections you submit.
Usage statistics — streak count, total tasks completed, total distractions logged.
This data exists solely to deliver the core functionality of the app. It is never used for advertising.

2.3 Voice and Audio (Brain Dump)
When you use the Brain Dump feature, your device microphone records an audio clip. That clip is transmitted over an encrypted connection to a secure server function, which forwards it to Groq for transcription. The transcribed text is returned to you for review before any tasks are created.

Audio recordings are not stored. The clip is discarded immediately after transcription. Groq's use of this data is governed by Groq's Privacy Policy.

2.4 App Preferences
Accent colour, haptic feedback preference, sound preference, morning and evening reminder settings, and biometric lock preference are stored against your account so they sync across reinstalls.

2.5 Subscription and Purchase Information
Subscription management is handled by RevenueCat. We receive only your subscription status (Free / Pro / Unlimited) and an anonymised identifier. Payment card details are never received or stored — those remain with Apple or Google.

2.6 Local Device Storage
A monthly brain-dump usage counter is stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is used only to enforce free-tier limits.

2.7 Biometric Authentication
If you enable biometric lock (Face ID / Touch ID), authentication is performed entirely by your device's operating system. No biometric data is accessed, received, or stored by Waffli.

4. Data Storage and Security
Your data is stored in Supabase-hosted databases protected by row-level security — only your authenticated account can read or write your data. All data is transmitted over TLS/HTTPS. We apply industry-standard security practices, though no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention
Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account (via Profile → Delete Account), all personal data — including tasks, mood entries, and preferences — is permanently deleted. This action is irreversible.
